Why Math?

Math is for everybody, not only for engineers. It doesn't matter what you want to do in the future. In the age of AI and Data, understanding mathmatics, can give you guidance in your job, finance, everyday matters and security. Opportunities in Math is going to appear in different forms and it may not appear as the math you see in your textbook, but it is important to recognize the math and link it to the concepts you learned that can then help you understand and maneuver your world better. Don't wait for math to come to you. Go get it and use it.

How math can help people

The UN is using blockchain, the data technology that supports Bitcoin, at refugee camps. It is "used to deliver aid more efficiently by dramaticaaly lowering the risk of bribes and fraud by local officials ---a huge, longstanding probelm in the industry". source Great, the technology is cool. Whoever wrote the codes for the system is great. But, hey, who came up with such an awesome idea? They are people who are on the ground, at the refugee camps, seeing the challenges everyday. That is what is great about these people. They may not be IT engineers, but they know the technology well, and they know the algorithm(which is math!) behind the latest technology. That is why you can "come up with" the idea to use blockchain for refugee assistance. You may be able to work in this industry without any knowledge of math or science, but when you know it, you can make a great difference.
